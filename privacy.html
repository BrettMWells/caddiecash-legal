Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Caddie Cash does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game data — players, scores, rounds, and course information — is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device except to sync to your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled.

iCloud Sync

If enabled, your game data syncs privately through Apple's CloudKit to your personal iCloud account. Caddie Cash has no access to this data. Apple's privacy policy applies to iCloud storage.

In-App Purchases

Caddie Cash Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all purchases.

Analytics

Caddie Cash does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not know how you use the app.

Contact

If you have privacy questions email: caddiecash.help@gmail.com
